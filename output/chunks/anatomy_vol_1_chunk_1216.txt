Figure 11.33 Muscles That Move the Feet and Toes

The muscles in the anterior compartment of the leg: the tibialis anterior, a long and thick muscle on the lateral
surface of the tibia, the extensor hallucis longus, deep under it, and the extensor digitorum longus, lateral to it, all
contribute to raising the front of the foot when they contract.

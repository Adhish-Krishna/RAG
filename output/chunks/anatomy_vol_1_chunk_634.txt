The skull is formed by 22 bones. Also associated with the head are an additional seven bones, including the hyoid bone
and the ear ossicles (three small bones found in each middle ear). The vertebral column consists of 24 bones, each called a
vertebra, plus the sacrum and coccyx. The thoracic cage includes the 12 pairs of ribs, and the sternum, the flattened bone
of the anterior chest.

For example, the radial nerve contains fibers of cutaneous sensation in the arm,
as well as motor fibers that move muscles in the arm.

 Spinal nerves of the thoracic region, T2 through T11, are not part of the plexuses but rather emerge and give rise to the
intercostal nerves found between the ribs, which articulate with the vertebrae surrounding the spinal nerve.

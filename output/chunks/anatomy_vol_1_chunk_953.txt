Knee Joint

The knee joint is the largest joint of the body (Figure 9.19). It actually consists of three articulations. The femoropatellar
joint is found between the patella and the distal femur. The medial tibiofemoral joint and lateral tibiofemoral joint
are located between the medial and lateral condyles of the femur and the medial and lateral condyles of the tibia.

As the spinal cord continues to develop in
the newborn, anatomical features mark its surface. The anterior midline is marked by the anterior median fissure, and the
posterior midline is marked by the posterior median sulcus. Axons enter the posterior side through the dorsal (posterior)
nerve root, which marks the posterolateral sulcus on either side. The axons emerging from the anterior side do so through
the ventral (anterior) nerve root. Note that it is common to see the terms dorsal (dorsal = “back”) and ventral (ventral =
